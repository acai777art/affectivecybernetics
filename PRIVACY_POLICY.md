## 2. Data Processing Overview

When you interact with ACAI, certain data may be processed to enable its functionality.

This may include:

- Telegram user identifiers (such as user ID, username, first name, and last name, as provided by the Telegram API);
- Messages sent to the bot, including text and voice messages;
- Automatically generated analytical signals derived from messages (such as tone, emotional valence, intensity, sarcasm, or risk indicators);
- User-uploaded documents or files (if applicable), processed solely to provide user-initiated knowledge retrieval features;
- Technical metadata (timestamps, usage metrics, system logs).

ACAI does not intentionally collect sensitive personal data.

## 3. Purpose of Data Use

Data is processed strictly for:

- enabling conversational functionality and continuity;

Data is not sold, not rented, not shared for advertising, and not used for marketing or profiling purposes.
