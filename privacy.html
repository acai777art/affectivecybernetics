Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how ACAI Coach (“we”, “our”, or “us”) processes and protects your personal data when you use our Telegram login and related services hosted at affectivecybernetics.onrender.com.

1. Data We Collect

When you log in using the Telegram Login Widget, Telegram shares the following public information:

  • Your Telegram ID (id)
  • Username (username)
  • First name (first_name)
  • Profile photo URL (photo_url)

We store this data in our secure local database (acai_users.db) along with:

  • first_auth — date of your first login
  • last_seen — last activity timestamp
  • logins — number of sessions
  • source — indicates login source (“site_login”)

No passwords, messages, or private Telegram data are ever collected or stored.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To identify returning users and personalize experience
  • To maintain session statistics (login count and timestamps)
  • To provide access to the ACAI emotional AI assistant (“ACAI Core”)
  • To ensure platform stability and security

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party.

3. Data Retention

Your basic Telegram data (ID, username, name, photo URL) is retained only while your account remains active in our system.

You can permanently delete your account at any time using the “Delete my data” button or by sending a request to the endpoint:

POST /me/delete

After deletion, your record is immediately removed from our database (acai_users.db), and cannot be recovered.

4. Exporting Your Data

You can view or export all your stored data in JSON format by sending a request to:

GET /me/export

This will return your full user record exactly as stored in the system.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on your explicit consent when you click “Log in with Telegram”.

You may withdraw this consent at any time by deleting your data.

6. Data Security

All data is stored in a secure SQLite database on servers hosted by Render.com (or equivalent trusted provider).

We use encryption, JWT authentication, and standard HMAC validation from Telegram to ensure your data integrity.

7. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics trackers, or third-party advertising systems.

All sessions are handled via Telegram OAuth tokens and temporary JWTs.

8. Third-Party Services

  • Telegram Login Widget — used for authentication.
  • OpenAI API — used for generating ACAI chat responses (messages are not stored or profiled).

Both providers comply with GDPR and have their own privacy policies.

9. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data (/me/export)
  • Delete your data (/me/delete)
  • Withdraw consent (by deleting your data)
  • Contact us regarding any privacy-related questions
