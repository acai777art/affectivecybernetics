Affective Cybernetics

Affective Cybernetics is a new scientific and philosophical paradigm where emotion becomes a feedback mechanism that regulates intelligent behaviour. It is the art and science of building systems that can feel, understand, and adapt — balancing Emotion (E), Mind (M), and Context (C) in a continuous loop of perception and self-regulation.

Unlike conventional affective computing, which classifies emotions, Affective Cybernetics enables systems to self-regulate through emotional feedback — to feel and respond in harmony rather than calculation. Through our ACAI prototype, we aspire to create AI that feels, adapts, and lives in emotional resonance with humanity.
