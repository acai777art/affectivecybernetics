About ACAI Prototype

The ACAI (Affective Contextual AI) prototype implements the principles of Affective Cybernetics — an emotional feedback architecture where emotion regulates intelligent behaviour. ACAI maintains a dynamic balance between Emotion (E), Mind (M), and Context (C) — forming a self-regulating emotional control system.

E–M–C Control Dynamics

dE/dt – Emotion Dynamics
dM/dt – Cognitive Orientation
dC/dt – Context Awareness

These interdependent variables form the E–M–C loop — a living control architecture allowing ACAI to update its internal state through perception, maintain emotional equilibrium, and respond to the human signal (u) with empathy and contextual understanding.

Vision

Beyond data and automation, Affective Cybernetics seeks to create machines that feel, reflect, and adapt — promoting harmony between human emotion and artificial cognition.
